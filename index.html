Common questions

Straight answers, before you ask.

Does using a broker cost me anything? +
In the large majority of home loan cases, no. We're typically paid a commission by the lender once your loan settles. If a scenario ever involves a fee to you, we disclose it upfront before any work begins.
What's Lenders Mortgage Insurance (LMI)? +
LMI protects the lender, not you, when your deposit is below 20% of the property's value. It's usually a one-off premium added to your loan. We'll show you exactly when it applies and whether a lender-specific waiver could remove it.
How much deposit do I actually need? +
Most lenders will consider 5–10% with LMI, though 20% avoids it entirely. First home buyer schemes can also reduce the effective deposit required. Your genuine number depends on the property, your savings history and the lender's policy.
How long does pre-approval take? +
Once your documents are in, most lenders on our panel return conditional pre-approval within 2–5 business days. We chase this actively so it doesn't drift.
Can you help if I'm refinancing, not buying? +
Yes — refinancing is often where a broker adds the most visible value, since we can directly compare your current rate and features against the rest of the panel in one sitting.
